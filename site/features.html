Unique Features

  • Deep Evolutionary Trait System: Modular genetic tree with visible, inheritable traits and mutations.
  • Emergent Generational Storytelling: Every pop is unique, with their own traits, relationships, and stories.
  • Procedural Language Evolution: Tribes develop unique languages you can decipher and influence.
  • Branching Tribal Legacies: Guide tribes to split, adapt, and form new cultures in diverse biomes.
  • Accessible Complexity: Deep simulation with intuitive controls and clear UI.
