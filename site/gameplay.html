Core Gameplay Pillars

Survive

Manage needs, gather resources, build shelter, and protect your tribe from threats.

Evolve

Earn Evolution Points to unlock and enhance genetic traits, shaping your lineage across generations.

Branch

Guide tribe splits and adaptation, creating new cultures and legacies in different environments.

Legacy

Track family trees, milestones, and traditions as your civilization grows and stories unfold.
