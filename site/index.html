Lineage: Ancestral Legacies

Overview Features Gameplay Progression Procedural Language Roadmap

Guide your tribe. Shape their destiny. Forge a legacy.

Lineage: Ancestral Legacies is an evolutionary RTS and colony simulation where you nurture a tribe of early hominids, guiding them from survival to civilization through genetics, culture, and emergent storytelling.

Game Overview

  • Genre: Evolutionary RTS / Colony Simulation Strategy
  • Perspective: 2D Top-Down Pixel Art
  • Platform: PC (Windows, Mac/Linux planned)
  • Engine: GameMaker Studio 2
  • Audience: Strategy & simulation fans, emergent storytelling lovers, and legacy-builders

Unique Features

  • Deep Evolutionary Trait System: Modular genetic tree with visible, inheritable traits and mutations.
  • Emergent Generational Storytelling: Every pop is unique, with their own traits, relationships, and stories.
  • Procedural Language Evolution: Tribes develop unique languages you can decipher and influence.
  • Branching Tribal Legacies: Guide tribes to split, adapt, and form new cultures in diverse biomes.
  • Accessible Complexity: Deep simulation with intuitive controls and clear UI.

Core Gameplay Pillars

Survive

Manage needs, gather resources, build shelter, and protect your tribe from threats.

Evolve

Earn Evolution Points to unlock and enhance genetic traits, shaping your lineage across generations.

Branch

Guide tribe splits and adaptation, creating new cultures and legacies in different environments.

Legacy

Track family trees, milestones, and traditions as your civilization grows and stories unfold.

Progression Flow

  1. Dawn Age: Basic survival, foraging, and first discoveries.
  2. Primitive Age: Tool crafting, group hunting, and simple shelters.
  3. Tribal Age: Role specialization, communal structures, and early culture.
  4. Divergence Age: Environmental adaptation, unique cultures, and meta-genetics.
  5. Ancestral Legacy: Interconnected lineages, advanced research, and grand aspirations.

Procedural Language System

Each tribe invents and evolves its own language. Decipher words, influence vocabulary, and experience emergent linguistic drift. The language system is a living record of your tribe's journey, offering unique puzzles and cultural depth every playthrough.
