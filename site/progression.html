Progression Flow

  1. Dawn Age: Basic survival, foraging, and first discoveries.
  2. Primitive Age: Tool crafting, group hunting, and simple shelters.
  3. Tribal Age: Role specialization, communal structures, and early culture.
  4. Divergence Age: Environmental adaptation, unique cultures, and meta-genetics.
  5. Ancestral Legacy: Interconnected lineages, advanced research, and grand aspirations.
