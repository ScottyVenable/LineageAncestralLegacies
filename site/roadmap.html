Development Roadmap

  1. Core Prototype: Pop FSM, basic movement, simple foraging, minimal UI.
  2. Alpha: Expanded FSM, inventory, basic crafting, needs system, simple combat, genetic tree UI.
  3. Beta: Full FSM, roles, biomes, expanded genetics, advanced crafting, social dynamics, language system, tribe splitting, sound & music.
  4. Release Candidate: Full progression, advanced systems, inter-tribe dynamics, polish, tutorial, balancing.
  5. Post-Release: Content updates, new biomes, advanced features, modding, multiplayer.
